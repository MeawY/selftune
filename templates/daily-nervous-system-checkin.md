SelfTune is an open-source personal self-reflection toolkit for observing patterns in daily wellness, nervous-system regulation, recovery, supplements, wearable data, and subjective body awareness.

It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, or treatment system.

The goal is to help individuals and practitioners organize daily observations into structured Markdown logs, AI-assisted reviews, and gentle self-care reflections.
