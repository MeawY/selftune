## Example Weekly Review

Possible non-medical observations:

- Lower sleep scores appeared near lower Body Battery, more tension, and lower calm ratings.
- Delayed meals, late caffeine, and high workload appeared near more activated notes.
- Regular meals, morning light, hydration, gentle movement, and lower evening stimulation appeared near steadier notes.
- The new supplement on Day 3 cannot be interpreted because sleep, food timing, exercise, and workload also changed.
- A gentle next observation could be tracking lunch timing, caffeine timing, and evening screen load for one week.

Safe interpretation:

"This week, recovery capacity appeared stronger on days with longer sleep, regular meals, morning light, and lower evening stimulation. Activation appeared more often on days with shorter sleep, delayed meals, and higher workload. These are reflection patterns, not medical conclusions."
