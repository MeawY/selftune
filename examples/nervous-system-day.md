- Alcohol: none

## Nervous-System Check-In

- Current state: activated in the late morning, shut down in the late afternoon, steadier after dinner
- Body signals: tight jaw, shallow breathing, low afternoon energy
- Emotional tone: urgency, then fogginess
- Possible context: shorter sleep, coffee before a work call, delayed lunch, long screen session
- Support that helped: short walk, dinner, lower screen brightness, quiet time

## AI Review Example

Non-medical observations:

- The record suggests a shift from activation to lower-energy shutdown across the day.
- Shorter sleep, caffeine timing, work pressure, delayed lunch, and screen load may be useful context to track together.
- The walk and quiet evening routine may be worth noting as possible supports.
- This record does not show enough information to claim a cause.
- A small next observation could be recording caffeine timing, lunch timing, and activation level for three days.
- If the activation, shutdown, or distress feels severe, persistent, worsening, or unsafe, it may be worth discussing with a qualified professional.
