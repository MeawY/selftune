# Supplement Nervous-System Workflow

This workflow explains how to record supplements and nutrition as context for nervous-system reflection.

SelfTune does not evaluate supplement safety, prescribe dosage, recommend combinations, or suggest treatment. Supplement and nutrition notes are used only for pattern awareness.

## Inputs

Use:

- Supplement name
- Amount, if known
- Time taken
- Taken with or without food
- Reason for taking it
- Caffeine and alcohol
- Hydration
- Meals and protein context
- Digestion
- Sleep, Garmin data, mood, energy, and body signals

## What To Observe

Track supplements alongside:

- Sleep quality
- Stress load
- Recovery capacity
- Body Battery
- HRV trend, if available
- Digestion
- Body tension
- Energy
- Emotional tone
- Felt safety or calm

## Safe Questions

- Did I change more than one thing today?
- Did supplement timing change at the same time as sleep, caffeine, workload, exercise, or meals?
- Is this pattern worth tracking for a few more days?
- Is there a safety, dosage, interaction, pregnancy-related, or medication question that belongs with a qualified professional?

## Unsafe Questions

Avoid:

- "Which supplement fixed this?"
- "What dose should I take?"
- "Can I combine these?"
- "Can I stop medication?"
- "What condition does this supplement response prove?"

## Weekly Use

At the end of the week, review supplement notes as context only. Look for repeated timing patterns, missing context, and questions for professional support.
